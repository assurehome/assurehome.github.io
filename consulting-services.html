Architectural Designs

Assure Homes Architectural Services include designing,obtainingend-to end documents required for construction along with project administration. Further more, we also provide services such as feasibility study, project management and architectural programming.

Outline of services offered by Assure Homes in-house consultants:

Project management
Initial phase of your project, where the planning begins. You may provide your project needs, dreams and goals.
Design
During this stage, we can expect preliminary design sketches based on your requirements and budget to explore best options. Our Team will review with you by providing you a detail input outlining the merits of design
Documentation
Once we get a nod from you on design of your choice, our architect shall work on specifications and drawings. This stage is important as it helps you obtain permits and documents for construction.
On-site Administration
Assure Homes architect shall consult and advise you during construction by making on-site visits. This will ensure if construction is proceeding in right direction as per the documents and supervise the quality of work is maintained.
